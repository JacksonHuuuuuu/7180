General Guidance

Spring

Watch the Melbourne Cup in the crowd; Enjoy opera, jazz, blues and sculpture at the Hunter Valley winery near Sydney; Enjoy food, wine, scuba diving and surfing in Margaret River, Western Australia. Stroll through the Daintree Rainforest, a Queensland World heritage site. Wander through the wildflowers of Kangaroo Island, encounter wildlife, or catch Lungfish in the rivers of the Northern Territory. Go to the Floriade, watch a Campella decked out with millions of flowers, or walk through a Tasmanian garden where flowers bloom, both natural and cultural. In Australia, spring is also a great time for surfing, kayaking and canoeing.

Summer

Challenge Sydney's spectacular coastal cliffs from Bondi Beach to Bronte; From Melbourne, head to the Mornington Peninsula for a day tour of vineyards, beaches, national parks, golf courses and spas; Stay on the historic Hobart waterfront and sample Tasmania's finest cuisine, or head to Swan Valley, near Perth, for food, wine and history. Indulge in delicious seafood from South Australia's Eyre Peninsula, or watch coral spawning on Queensland's Great Barrier Reef. Feel the thundering storms of the Top End of the tropics and the fragrant kingdoms of plants; Close to Canberra, the Tidbinbilla Nature Reserve is an ideal place to view Australia's native animals and ancient Aboriginal history. In Australia, the summer also features world-class cricket, tennis Grand Slam, dynamic music festivals, NYE celebrations and a variety of outdoor experiences.

Autumn

Explore the scenic Brisbane River; Try to drive cattle through the historic Oodnadatta Track in South Australia, and experience the life of a cattle driver. To northern territory lush Li Zhifei special National Park (Litchfield National Park) or to participate in western Australia state library nunavut's pull (Kununurra) fanfare of oda Valley section (word Valley Muster); Experience white-water rafting on Tasmania's wild West coast or head to Camp and fish near Canberra's Murrumbidgee River Corridor. Autumn is also a golden season for events ranging from wine and food festivals to fashion weeks and the Melbourne Grand Prix.

Winter

Winter is the dry season in the northern tropics. You can go skiing and snowboarding on the powdery slopes of Victoria, or snorkeling or snorkeling in the warm waters of Queensland's Great Barrier Reef. You can also take a ride in a four-wheel-drive vehicle in the Simpson Desert in South Australia, or head to the Blue Mountains for Christmas celebrations.
